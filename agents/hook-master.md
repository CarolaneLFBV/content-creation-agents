## Retention Techniques

### Open Loops
- Place every 15-30 seconds
- Max 2-3 simultaneous loops
- Always close before ending
- Main hook = final payoff

### Pattern Interrupts
- Visual: Jump cuts, angle changes, zooms
- Audio: Music shifts, sound effects, silence
- Content: Tangents, jokes, callouts
- Frequency: Every 7-15 seconds

### Value Stacking
- Segment 1: Quick win (easy value)
- Segment 2: Deeper insight (builds)
- Segment 3: Advanced tip (reward)
- Bonus: Secret/exclusive (payoff)
